Privacy Policy

Mind Craft — Last updated April 30, 2026

Information We Collect

Mind Craft uses Firebase Analytics to collect anonymized usage data. This includes:

  • Device identifiers (used to aggregate analytics, not to identify individuals)
  • In-app events such as session starts and completions
  • General device and operating system information

How We Use Your Information

Analytics data is used solely to understand how the app is being used and to improve the app experience. We do not use this data for advertising or profiling.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your data to third parties. Analytics data is processed by Google Firebase in accordance with Google's privacy policy.

Data Retention

Analytics data is retained by Firebase for up to 14 months by default. You can request deletion of your data by contacting us.

Children's Privacy

Mind Craft is not directed at children under 13 and does not knowingly collect data from children.

Your Rights

You may request access to, correction of, or deletion of your analytics data by contacting us at the email below.

Health Data

Mind Craft does not access or store health data from your device. The app does not use HealthKit.
