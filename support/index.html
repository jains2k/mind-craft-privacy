Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create a custom meditation routine? Tap New Routine on the home screen, then add blocks — choose from Breathwork, Meditation, Guided Visualization, or Shavasana. Drag the handles to reorder them, then tap Save.
Why isn't audio playing during my session? Check that your iPhone is not in Silent mode (the physical switch on the side). Also verify that Mind Craft has permission to play audio in the background under Settings → Mind Craft. If the issue persists, close and reopen the app.
Will a session keep playing if I lock my screen? Yes. Mind Craft requests background audio playback so your session continues uninterrupted when the screen locks. You can also control playback from the Lock Screen.
How do I set daily reminders? Open Settings (the gear icon) and tap Reminders. You can add one or more reminders at your preferred times. Mind Craft will ask for notification permission the first time you set one.
How do I edit or delete a routine? On the Routines screen, swipe left on a routine to reveal the Delete button. To edit, tap the routine and then the Edit button at the top of the detail screen.
Can I use the app without an internet connection? Yes. All routines, audio, and guided meditations are stored on your device. No internet connection is required to meditate.
